Welcome to My Food Page!

Why this page?

As a busy college student, I struggle to make the time to cook, which leads to unhealthy eating habits...

How will this page help me?

I struggle with ideas of things to cook, or when I go to the store I forget what ingrediants I would want. By creating a page that has a few easy, healthy recipes for me to follow, I hope to start saving money and creating better eating habits for myself!

Some recipes I've created!!

How will I keep myself accountable?

Every time that I make one of these recipes, I will upload a review of the food as well as an image of my own cooking!
